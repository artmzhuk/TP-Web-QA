This is a direct answer to your own question but isn't elevated to a high enough level. dplyr is truly an extendable front-end to multiple data storage mechanisms where as data.table is an extension to a single one.

Look at dplyr as a back-end agnostic interface, with all of the targets using the same grammer, where you can extend the targets and handlers at will. data.table is, from the dplyr perspective, one of those targets.
